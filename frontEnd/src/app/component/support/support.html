How do I book a ride?

Simply log in to your account, enter your pickup and destination locations, and confirm your booking. Our drivers will be notified immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash payments, mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), and credit/debit cards for your convenience.

How can I become a driver?

To become a driver, register with the "Register as Driver" option and complete our verification process. We'll contact you within 24 hours.

What if my driver doesn't show up?

If your driver doesn't arrive within 5 minutes of the scheduled time, please contact our support team immediately for assistance.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 5 minutes before the scheduled pickup time without any cancellation fees.

Is my ride safe?

Absolutely! All our drivers are verified, licensed, and insured. We also provide real-time tracking for your safety.
